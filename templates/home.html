About

This project is designed by 3 engineering students: Esha Sachan, Prashasti Gupta and Ruchika Mishra. The main idea behind ConvoCare is to use technology to know about people's inner feelings based on the texts they use anywhere.

ConvoCare aims at improving relationships between people ,organizations , customers by analyzing the hidden emotion in the texts for clearer interpretation.This type of sentiment analysis aims to detect emotions, like happiness, frustration, anger, sadness, and so on.
