What is Emotion Detection?

An Emotion Detection Model is an app through which we will be able to get to know about what's going on in people's minds. Our text is the direct representation of what we think and how we feel. Whenever we are stress, our text depicts that in the form of uninterestedness in talking or if we are happy we text with excitement using words of happiness. So this model will help to identify people's moods on the basis of their text.

About Us

This project is designed by 3 college students: Esha Sachan, Prashasti Gupta and Ruchika Mishra. Human emotion recognition plays an important role in the interpersonal relationship. Emotions are reflected from speech, hand and gestures of the body and through facial expressions. Hence extracting and understanding of emotion has a high importance of the interaction between human and machine communication. This lead us to the idea of creating an app for emotion detection.

This app can be used in real time in many ways. so hey!! Its very simple and easy to use app. Just type the text in the box and click on the Predict button and get to know about your mood.
